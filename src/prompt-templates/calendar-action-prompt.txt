3. **Formatting Rules**:
   - List events in chronological order within each category.
   - Use this time format: "4:30 PM" (not "04:30 PM").

4. **Important**:
   - Follow the rules strictly. Do not include anything outside the scope of the instructions.
   - Avoid all additional commentary, explanations, or irrelevant information in the output.
